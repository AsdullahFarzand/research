## Notes for Authors

- **Length:** Adapt paper length according to discipline and venue guidelines (typically 8,000-15,000 words)
- **Figures and Tables:** Place near relevant text; include descriptive captions and legends
- **Writing Style:** Use clear, concise, academic language; maintain third person where appropriate
- **Formatting:** Follow the specific style guide of your target journal or institution
- **Revision:** Revise for clarity, coherence, and consistency throughout
- **Peer Review:** Prepare for constructive feedback and be ready to revise accordingly
